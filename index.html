NU Quantum-AI Hackathon

Current practical Quantum Computers have a strong potential to enhance AI through hybrid classical-Quantum systems. That hybrid system fits nicely with the current Quantum technology limitations. This event aims to introduce this emerging paradigm to Egypt, through Hackathon-based learning. We only assume an AI development background and zero Quantum Computing knowledge!

Date: During the first week of July 2024

Duration of the Hackathon: 2 Days

Location: Nile University

FAQ

What is the Nile University Quantum-AI Hackathon?

The Nile University Quantum-AI Hackathon is an event designed to introduce participants to the emerging field of Quantum Computing integrated with AI. It aims to build a thriving Quantum Community in Egypt, democratize Quantum Computing knowledge, and foster innovation.

Who can participate?

The Hackathon is open to students, professionals, and anyone interested in technology and innovation.

Do I need a team?

You can register with a team or join a team during the event.

Is there a registration fee?

No, the Hackathon is free to attend.

What should I expect to learn from the Hackathon?

Participants will gain hands-on experience with Quantum Computing and AI tools, develop practical skills, and explore potential real-world applications of quantum-AI in various domains.

Do I need prior knowledge of Quantum Computing to participate?

No prior knowledge of Quantum Computing is required. The Hackathon assumes participants have a background in AI development but zero knowledge of Quantum Computing.
