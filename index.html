About the Hackathon

Current practical Quantum Computers have a strong potential to enhance AI through hybrid classical-Quantum systems. That hybrid system fits nicely with the current Quantum technology limitations. This event aims to introduce this emerging paradigm to Egypt, through hackathon-based learning. We only assume an AI development background and zero Quantum Computing knowledge!

Date: During the first week of July 2024

Duration of the Hackathon: 2 Days

Location: Nile University

FAQ

What is a Hackathon?

A hackathon is a social coding event where programmers, designers, and others collaborate intensively on software projects.

Who can participate?

The hackathon is open to students, professionals, and anyone interested in technology and innovation.

Do I need a team?

You can register with a team or join a team during the event.

Is there a registration fee?

No, the hackathon is free to attend.
